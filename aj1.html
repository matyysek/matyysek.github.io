Česká republika

The Czech Republic is small country in Europe(continent).
Its area is 78 864 square kilometers.
It is situated in the middle-centre of Europe.

The country is separated into 3 parts: Bohemia in the west, Moravia in the east and Silesia in the northern part of Moravia
It has got mild climate - we have got 4 seasons: spring, summer, autumn(fall) and winter.
Its surface is flat(lowlands, valleys) or hilly(highlands, mountains).
The highest peak is Sněžka in the Krkonoše Mountains.

Our neighbours are:
1. Poland to the north
2. Germany to the west
3. Austria to the south
4. Slovakia to the east

The longest river is Vltava which sprinte in the Šumava Mountains and flows into the Labe river. Other important rivers are Labe, Odra and Morava

The population of the Czech Republic is 10,3 million inhabitants. Its density is 240 people to 1 square kilometer.
The main nationality is Czech, but there are some minorities like Slovaks, polish, Germans, Russians and members of other nations.

The most populated cities are Prague, Brno, Ostrava, Plzeň for example.
Prague is the capital city of the country

The Czech Republic was set up on the 1st January 1993. It is a parliamentary democracy with the president as the head of the state.
The president is elected every 5 years by the parliament.
The parliament consists of 2 houses - The Chamber of Deputies and the Senate.

The main industries are chemical(in Ústí nad Labem, Lovosice, Neratovice, Kralupy nad Vltavou)
textile(in Liberec, Prostějov, Praha, Zlín)
food(in all big cities)
breweries(Plzeň, České Budějovice, Kláštěr)
glass(Karlovy Vary, Nový Bor)
matches(Sušice)
wood and paper.
There are deposits of brown, black coal and iron ore too.

Our country is a tourist place as well. A lot of foreigners come to see the sight in Prague like Charles Bridge, Prague Castle, the National Theatre.
Other interesting places are in these cities: Brno, České Budějovice, Karlovy Vary, Český Krumlov and others.

Agricultural production is situated mainly in lowlands(e.g. Polabská).
The main produces are grains, suger-beet, maize, hop, sunflower, fruit.
Potatoes and animals(sheep, cows) can be found in the highlands.

That is all about the country where we live.
